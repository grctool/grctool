## Intent Summary

GRCTool is transitioning from a Tugboat Logic aggregator to a **system of record** for compliance data. The planning stack defines:

- **Vision**: Master index with GRCTool-native IDs, bidirectional integration, data sovereignty, plugin architecture
- **Features**: FEAT-004 (provider framework, P0), FEAT-001 (AccountableHQ), FEAT-002 (GDrive), FEAT-003 (lifecycle/scheduler)
- **Architecture**: ADR-010 (system of record), ADR-011 (universal document provider), ADR-006 (hexagonal architecture)
- **Designs**: SD-004 (DataProvider/SyncProvider interfaces), SD-001/002/003 (adapter implementations)
